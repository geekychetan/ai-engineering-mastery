# Project Brief — <Project Name>

> Write this **before** you build. It's the habit that makes you sound beyond junior.

## 1. The problem
- **Who is the user?**
- **What problem do they have?** (in their words)
- **How is it solved today?** (the painful status quo)

## 2. Why AI?
- Why is an LLM/ML approach a good fit here (vs plain code/rules)?
- What's the *simplest* version that could work?

## 3. Approach
- High-level design (RAG? agent? plain prompt? classifier?).
- Key components and data flow.
- Model + tools chosen, and **why** (cost/latency/quality tradeoff).

## 4. Success metric — how will I measure "good"?
- What does success look like, concretely?
- How will I evaluate it? (golden set, LLM-as-judge, accuracy, user task completion...)
- What's the baseline to beat?

## 5. Risks / what could go wrong
- Failure modes (hallucination, cost blowups, latency, bad inputs).
- How I'll mitigate or at least detect them.
